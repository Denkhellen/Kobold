WELCOME TO THE WORLD OF THE LITTLE ONES.

Enter the enchanted realm where grass blades sway like skyscrapers and tiny beings dance under the moonlit canopy, welcoming you to the world of the little ones.

In this miniature universe, each dewdrop is a pond and each flower a fortress. Peek behind the veil and immerse yourself in the whimsical wonder of the small beings that call it home.

As you tread softly through dew-kissed meadows, a hidden world reveals itself, where whispers of tiny creatures and rustling of tiny wings invite you to explore the magic of the small.

Places where you meet us.

Library

Unique Location: Mystic Meadow, 24 Elderwood Grove.

Local Spot: Popular among the goblin community.

Hours: Open from dawn till dusk.

Features: Enchanted books, tranquil setting.

Directions: Follow twinkling lights on Glitter Path.

Café

Charming Venue: Floral Meadow Café, 12 Blossom Bend.

Goblin Favorite: Frequented by flower-loving goblins.

Operating Hours: Sunrise to sunset service.

Key Features: Rustic, nature-inspired décor.

Directions: Near the Dewdrop Fountain, follow the petal trail.

Parking Area

Location: Meadow Park, 77 Greenleaf Lane.

Popular Spot: Chosen by nature-loving goblins.

Hours: Available from dusk till dawn.

Features: Eco-friendly, blends with nature.

Directions: Past the Whispering Willows, turn at the third sunflower.

Outdoor Cinema

Venue: Starlight Cinema, 33 Moonbeam Terrace.

Local Favorite: Loved by movie-going goblins.

Hours: Nightly screenings, after sunset.

Special Features: Under-the-stars experience.

Directions: Follow the glowworm lights, near Silverstream River.
